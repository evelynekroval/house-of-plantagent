I found an easy Tofu Noodles with Almond Butter Sauce recipe at [Vancouver with Love](https://vancouverwithlove.com/almond-butter-tofu-noodles/). Here is the full recipe:

### Ingredients:
- **For the Tofu:**
  - 1 block of tofu, cut into 1-inch cubes
  - 1 tablespoon tamari or soy sauce
  - 1 tablespoon avocado oil
  - 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

- **For the Noodles:**
  - Noodles of your choice (cook as per package instructions)
  
- **For the Sauce:**
  - 2 tablespoons almond butter
  - 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  - 2 teaspoons maple syrup
  - 2 tablespoons tamari or soy sauce

- **For the Vegetables:**
  - 1/2 tablespoon oil (for cooking)
  - Mushroom slices
  - Bok choy
  - Carrot, sliced
  - Green onions (for garnish)
  - Cilantro leaves, toasted almond pieces or sesame seeds, and lime wedges (for serving)

### Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven's broiler (grill). Toss the tofu cubes in a pan with tamari, avocado oil, and apple cider vinegar. Broil for 10 minutes until golden, then flip and broil for another 10 minutes until browned but not tough. Remove from the oven and set aside.

2. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Rinse and drain excess water, then set aside.

3. In a small bowl, mix together the almond butter, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, and tamari until smooth to create the sauce.

4. In a large non-stick pan, heat the remaining oil over medium heat. Add the mushroom slices and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.

5. Add the bok choy and carrot to the pan and cook for another 3 minutes, continuing to stir.

6. Reduce the heat and add the pre-cooked noodles, tofu, and green onions to the skillet. Pour over the sauce and mix well to combine. Allow everything to cook for another minute or so until heated through.

7. Serve in large bowls, garnished with cilantro leaves, toasted almond pieces or sesame seeds, and lime wedges if desired.

Enjoy your delicious and easy tofu noodle dish!
